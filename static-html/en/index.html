Company Profile More

San Diego Agricultural Group, one of the world's top 500 companies, is located in Florida. It is a high-tech enterprise integrating scientific research, manufacturing, and international and domestic markets, focusing on life science industries such as environmental protection pesticides and healthy fertilizers. San Diego Agricultural Materials Group covers 68 countries and regions around the world. It entered the Chinese market in 2002, and its marketing headquarters is located in Zhengzhou, which has convenient transportation in all directions. At present, the Chinese market mainly includes counties and cities in Henan, Hebei, Anhui, Jiangsu, Shandong, Hubei and other provinces.

The company has always adhered to the principle of "earning reputation with quality and building brands with strength". Our company has established a technological innovation system with a scientific research center as the core, adhered to the combination of technology introduction, scientific research and technological transformation, and insisted on "production generation, Improvement generation, idea generation" product development ......
